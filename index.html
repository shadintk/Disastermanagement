What is Disaster Management?

Understanding the comprehensive approach to disaster preparedness and response

01

Prevention & Mitigation

Reducing the risk of disasters through proactive measures, early warning systems, and community education programs.

02

Preparedness

Developing response plans, conducting drills, and ensuring communities are ready to act when disasters strike.

03

Response

Immediate actions taken during and after a disaster to save lives, protect property, and meet basic human needs.

04

Recovery

Long-term efforts to restore and rebuild communities stronger and more resilient than before the disaster.
